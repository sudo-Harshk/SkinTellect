Privacy Policy

At SkinTellect, your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our AI-powered skincare services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile data (name, email, account details)
  • Images and analysis data submitted by users
  • Appointment and activity logs

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide accurate AI skincare insights
  • To personalize recommendations
  • To improve and enhance our algorithms
  • To communicate with you regarding appointments and updates

3. Data Protection

All sensitive information is encrypted and stored securely. We implement best-in-class security measures to prevent unauthorized access or misuse of data.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your data with third parties unless required by law or to deliver our core services (e.g., dermatology partners for booked appointments).

5. Your Rights

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your profile and records
  • Opt out of promotional communications

6. Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
